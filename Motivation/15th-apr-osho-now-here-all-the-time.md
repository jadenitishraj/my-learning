│   ├── Stop only when death takes away all possibility

│   └── In your very planning, you sow the seeds

│   ├── Live intensely and totally now

│   ├── Live it totally and joyously

│   ├── Present is so miserable he wants to avoid it

│   └── Present is the only time you have

│   ├── A "now" stretched from birth to death

│   ├── Here is the only space
│   └── Wherever you are, that place becomes here

    ├── Meet death full of joy, silence, serenity
    ├── Hands full of ecstasy
    ├── In that ecstasy, death itself dies
